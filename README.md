# CareVault

CareVault is a digital platform designed to help families securely store, organize, and manage their medical records in one centralized location. Instead of keeping prescriptions and reports scattered across different hospitals or paper files, CareVault provides a single place where users can upload and access important health documents anytime.

The platform allows users to maintain records for themselves and their family members, making it easier to track prescriptions, lab reports, and other medical documents. By organizing health information in a structured way, CareVault helps users quickly find important records whenever they need them.

In addition to storing documents, CareVault also provides simple AI-generated insights from uploaded medical reports. These insights help users better understand the information contained in their prescriptions or reports in a clear and easy-to-read format.

## Features

- Secure user registration and login  
- Upload and manage medical prescriptions and reports  
- Organize records for multiple family members  
- Dashboard to view uploaded documents  
- AI-generated summaries of medical reports  
- Option to delete uploaded files  
- User-specific access to ensure privacy and security  

## Purpose

Managing health records can often be difficult when documents are stored in multiple places. CareVault aims to simplify this by creating a centralized digital vault where families can safely store, organize, and access their medical information.

## Future Improvements
